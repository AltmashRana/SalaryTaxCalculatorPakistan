How the Optimizer Works

The Auto-Generator uses a standard 60% Basic Salary ratio. This helps in balancing your Provident Fund contributions while maintaining a healthy allowance structure for House Rent and Utilities.

Tax Exemption Logic

Medical Allowance up to 10% of Basic Salary is tax-free. Our tool automatically calculates this for you. Work Expenses are treated as direct reimbursements and are also excluded from your taxable income.

Provident Fund Impact

PF is calculated based on Basic Salary. While the Employer's contribution adds to your gross wealth, your own contribution is deducted from your take-home pay.
